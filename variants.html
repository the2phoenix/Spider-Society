CLASSIFICATION SYSTEM

CANON-COMPLIANT

Standard Spider-People following their designated timeline without deviation. Core powers: web-slinging, wall-crawling, spider-sense.

ALTERNATE POWERS

Variants with unique abilities beyond the standard set. Includes electricity manipulation (Miles), camouflage, venom strike, precognition.

CROSS-SPECIES

Spider-powered individuals with distinct origins. Different universes, timelines, or dimensional variants with unique cultural backgrounds.

HYBRIDS

Mixed powers or dual origins. Combination of Spider abilities with other power sources (symbiotes, genetic modifications, tech enhancements).

DIVERGENT

Those who broke from their canon events. High risk of dimensional instability. Constant monitoring required.

UNKNOWN ORIGIN

Mysterious or unclassified variants. Abilities don't match known Spider-Person profiles. Ongoing investigation.
