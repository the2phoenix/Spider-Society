MISSION BRIEF // LOGS

LOG 01: THE GREAT WEB // ORIGIN
The Multiverse is not a chaotic accident, but a structured neural network of reality known as the Great Web of Life and Destiny. It connects every spider-powered individual across infinite dimensions. We are the antibodies of the multiverse, tasked with protecting the strands that hold existence together. Without the Web, there is no reality. Without us, the Web unravels into nothingness.

LOG 02: ANOMALY DETECTION
When a variable from one dimension is forcefully displaced into another, it creates a rupture in the space-time continuum. These "Anomalies" threaten the stability of the host dimension. If left unchecked, a single anomaly can trigger a chain reaction, leading to a molecular collapse. Our primary directive is to locate, contain, and return these anomalies before critical mass is reached.

LOG 03: SOCIETY FOUNDATION
Founded in Earth-928 by Miguel O'Hara, the Spider-Society was established as a cross-dimensional task force. We operate outside of traditional jurisdiction, answerable only to the algorithm of the Web itself. Our headquarters serves as the central hub for all multiversal operations, housing the "Go-Home Machine" and the containment fields for captured villains who endanger the canon.

LOG 04: DIMENSIONAL WATCH TECHNOLOGY
Standard issue for all agents, the Dimensional Watch (Gizmo) is the key to our operations. It allows for safe travel between dimensions by stabilizing the user's atoms to match the vibrational frequency of the destination universe. Without this specialized technology, prolonged exposure to a foreign dimension results in cellular decay—a painful process known as "glitching."

LOG 05: CANON EVENT PROTOCOL
There are moments in time that are fixed—absolute points that define a Spider's journey. The bite, the loss of a loved one, the lesson of responsibility. These "Canon Events" are the structural load-bearing pillars of the multiverse. Disrupting them does not save lives; it destroys entire universes. We bear the burden of ensuring these tragedies occur, for the greater good of existence.

LOG 06: CELLULAR DESTABILIZATION
"Glitching" is the physical manifestation of dimensional rejection. The host universe attempts to purge foreign matter, causing violent digital artifacts and intense pain. It is a warning sign of imminent atomic separation. Agents must maintain their watch synchronization at all times. Failure to do so will result in the agent being scattered across the sub-atomic void.

LOG 07: CONTAINMENT & RETURN
The Go-Home Machine uses DNA matching to identify an individual's dimension of origin. Once an anomaly is captured, they are placed in the machine, which forcibly triangulates their home frequency and ejects them back to their native reality. This process is non-negotiable. The stability of the web takes precedence over individual desires or timelines.

LOG 08: ACTIVE RECRUITMENT
The fracturing of the multiverse has accelerated. We have detected a new, unknown variable—you. Your bio-signature matches the potential for recruitment. We need operators who can make the hard choices, who understand that being a hero isn't always about saving everyone. It's about saving the world. Welcome to the Spider-Society. Do not disappoint us.

SPIDER CLASSIFICATIONS

CANON-COMPLIANT

STATUS: PROTECTED

Spider-People who have experienced all required Canon Events. Their timelines are structurally sound and do not require monitoring. They form the backbone of the Web.

ANOMALIES

STATUS: CRITICAL THREAT

Individuals displaced from their native dimension. They destabilize the host universe, causing glitches and potential collapse. Immediate capture and return protocols apply.

VARIANTS

STATUS: MONITORED

Spider-People with non-standard abilities or origin stories. While their timelines remain intact, their unpredictability requires ongoing surveillance by the Society.

ROGUES

STATUS: HOSTILE

Former Society members or independents who actively resist our protocols. They prioritize individual desires over multiversal stability. Extremely dangerous.

RECRUITS

STATUS: UNDER EVALUATION

Newly identified Spider-People undergoing assessment for Society membership. Their abilities and dimensional stability are being monitored before full integration.

HYBRID CLASS

STATUS: SPECIAL CONTAINMENT

Spider-People with symbiote bonds, genetic mutations, or cross-dimensional fusion. Their unpredictable nature requires isolated monitoring protocols.
