WHAT ARE CANON EVENTS?

Canon Events are fixed points in time that define a Spider-Person's journey. These moments—both triumphant and tragic—are essential threads in the Web of Life and Destiny. They are not merely experiences; they are the very fabric that holds the multiverse together.

Every Spider-Person across every dimension must experience their own version of these events. Attempts to prevent or alter canon events result in dimensional instability, timeline collapse, and the potential destruction of entire universes.

THE THREAD CANNOT BE BROKEN. THE WEB MUST REMAIN INTACT.

⚠ DO NOT DISRUPT CANON EVENTS ⚠

Interfering with a canon event, no matter how noble the intention, creates a fracture in the dimensional fabric. These fractures propagate exponentially, destabilizing neighboring universes and threatening the entire multiverse.

CONSEQUENCE: DIMENSIONAL COLLAPSE

When a canon event is prevented, the affected Spider-Person's dimension begins to unravel. Reality fragments. Time loops. Matter dissolves. The universe ceases to exist.

If you detect a canon event disruption, report it immediately to Spider-Society Mission Control.
